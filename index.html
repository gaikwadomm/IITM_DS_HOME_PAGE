Introducing Our Flagship Program: BS in Robotics & Automation

Responding to industry demands, IIT Madras is proud to launch its new flagship 4-year Bachelor of Science (BS) Degree in Robotics & Automation. This cutting-edge program focuses on mechatronics, control systems, and autonomous navigation, with flexible exit points. This is a unique opportunity to specialize in a high-growth field with curriculum from a world-class institution, accessible globally. Join our exclusive first cohort of 5,000 students!
