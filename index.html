About Our Premier BS Degree in AI & Machine Learning

IIT Madras, a leader in global technical education, now offers a groundbreaking 4-year Bachelor of Science (BS) Degree in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning. This program is designed for the future, providing flexible exit options at the foundation, diploma, or BSc degree levels. This is a unique opportunity to earn a degree from a world-renowned IIT, accessible to anyone regardless of age or location. Join our growing community of over 45,000 ambitious learners!

New Featured Program: Quantum Computing

Prepare for the next technological revolution with our brand new certification in Quantum Computing. This 6-month intensive program covers quantum mechanics, algorithms like Shor's and Grover's, and hands-on experience with quantum simulators. Applications are now open for the inaugural batch starting Spring 2026.
